Quick Demo

1. Grab a sample problem and save on your computer.

curl https://www.coinsayer.com/problem.json > problem.json

2. Send the problem to coinsayer's api.

curl -H "Content-Type: application/json" --data @problem.json http://freeapi.coinsayer.com/v1/solve-problem

3. Analyze the output, use it to create the perfect bitcoin transaction!
