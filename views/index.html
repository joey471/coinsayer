What is coin selection?

When you send bitcoin, you must chose exactly which "inputs" to use. This has a profound effect on privacy and overall costs. And the amount of possible choices grows exponential with the amount of "coins" you have!

In fact, this problem is so hard that it is categorized as NP-hard, and in the general-case not really solvable.
